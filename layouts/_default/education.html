ME 658 Flight Stability and Control

The course covers basic topics in aircraft stability and control including:
  • Static and dynamic analysis of aircraft motion
  • Modeling and key stability derivatives
  • Classical and state space control techniques
  • Longitudinal autopilot
  • Lateral autopilot
Recommended Texts:
  • MIT Opencourseware 16-333 Notes by Prof. J. How
  • McLean's Automatic Flight Control Systems
